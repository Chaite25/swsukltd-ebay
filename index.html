HIKVISION COLORVU 5MP GREY IP CCTV CAMERA POE OUTDOOR 2.8MM COLOUR NIGHT-VISION

Product Description

HIKVISION COLORVU 5MP GREY IP CCTV CAMERA POE OUTDOOR 2.8MM COLOUR NIGHT-VISION

The HiLook IPC-T259H is a 5 MP IP POE turret camera with a 2.8mm fixed lens that utilises ColorVu technology to provide continuous full-color images (24/7).

H.265+ compression significantly reduces the amount of transmission bandwidth and storage space needed and PoE (Power over Ethernet) only needs a single cable for power and video, which speeds up and simplifies installation.

HiLook cameras with ColorVu technology can now capture full colour night time images.

Without using infrared, Colorvu technology is intended to produce crisp, colourful images in environments with little to no light. Large image sensors and sophisticated image processing are combined in Colorvu, to produce the best images possible in low-light situations.

The built-in white light of Colorvu Lite cameras can provide illumination in total darkness, enabling the camera to deliver full-color images 24 hours a day, no matter the lighting situation.

Having colour images makes it easier to identify subjects, capture licence plates, and provide precise context that is lost on traditional cameras.

  • This IP camera can be used with NVRs 5MP or higher.
  • The password for the camera must be the same as that of the NVR or the camera will show as offline.
  • We advise using a HiLook or Hikvision NVR for best results.
